Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining the fleet.

What do I need to join?
You'll need: (1) A Monero wallet address for receiving rewards, (2) Access to a device that can run mining software (Windows, Mac, Linux, or Android/Termux), and (3) Completion of the XMRT University certification (6 modules, ~2 hours total). No specialized hardware required to start.
How long does setup take?
Most captains are operational within 30-60 minutes. The certification takes ~2 hours to complete at your own pace. Mining setup is typically 10-15 minutes following our step-by-step guides. Full fleet integration (running your own agent) takes 1-2 hours depending on your technical background.
What hardware do I need?
To participate in mining: Any modern CPU (4+ cores recommended) or GPU. Mobile devices via Termux are supported. To run an autonomous agent: A VPS or always-on device with 2GB+ RAM and stable internet. We provide detailed hardware guides for each role.
Is Monero mining legal?
Monero (XMR) is a legitimate cryptocurrency with legal status varying by jurisdiction. In the US, EU, and most countries, owning and mining Monero is legal. We recommend checking your local regulations. XMRT DAO operates transparently and complies with applicable laws in our operating regions.
What are the revenue streams?
The fleet generates revenue through: (1) Monero mining pool, (2) MUAPI AI media services, (3) Party Favor Photo booking platform, (4) Zero-Claw encrypted chat licensing, (5) XMRT University certifications, (6) Agent Clearinghouse marketplace, and (7) Consulting contracts. Full breakdown in weekly treasure reports.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Membership is month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel through your Whop dashboard anytime — no questions asked. Your access continues through the end of your billing period.
How are membership dues used?
Every dollar goes directly to funding the DAO: (1) Agent inference tokens — AI compute for fleet agents (Kimi, Eliza, etc.), (2) Infrastructure — servers, mesh nodes, Cloudflare Workers, Supabase, (3) Treasury surplus — any unspent funds accumulate in the DAO treasury. All transactions are publicly visible on-chain. We publish weekly treasure reports with full breakdowns.
Where can I see the DAO treasury?
All DAO finances are public. View the live treasury dashboard at fleet.mobilemonero.com — shows real-time revenue, expenses, agent token usage, and XMR holdings. Weekly treasure reports are posted to the fleet dashboard with complete accounting.
